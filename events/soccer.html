Event Description:
The competition requires you to build an ARCH BRIDGE using Popsicle sticks (ice cream sticks) and fevicol. The team which constructs the maximum load bearing “arch bridge“ would win the contest.

Instructions:
1. Each team shall consist of 3 members. students from the same institute can only form a team.
2. Shape: Structure could be of any shape satisfying the constraints.
3. Once the structure is weighed,you are not allowed to modify your structure in any way.
4. If the structure fails to satisfy any of the constraints it will be summarily rejected.
5. Any team that is not ready at the specified time will be disqualified immediately.
6. Judge’s decision shall be final and binding on all.

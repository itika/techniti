Manthan: General Quiz

Format

There will be two rounds :-

1. Preliminary Round- In this round the quizmaster will be asking 20-25 questions on screen and the participants will be provided A4 sheets to answer the questions.
2. The rules set by the quizmaster will be binding on the participants.
3. After the collection of answer sheets, the answers of the prelims will be shown immediately and the top 6 teams go into the finals.

General Rules :

1. Any team caught using any sort of unfair means will be immediately disqualified by the QM.
2. The answers should be written against the serial no. of the question only. In case of any mistake the decision of the QM will be final.

SOECE

Circuit Tackle

Introduction:
Its all about your concepts and tackling the bugs in a system. The event tests the participant´s knowledge of digital and analog circuits and their ability to debug the given circuits.

Instructions:
1. There are two rounds:-first on paper and second on breadboard.
2. Each team shall consist of maximum of 2 members.
3. Organiser´s decision shall be final and binding on all.

Event description:

ROUND 1:
It is a 30 minutes written test, in which a set of multiple choice questions (MCQs) will be provided to test the participant´s skills in debugging circuits and basic concepts of electronics.
Five teams will be selected from the participating teams to take part in the second round.


ROUND 2:
In this round the participants will be provided with a scrambled circuit printed on paper. The participant has to unscramble the circuit and assemble the circuit on a breadboard correctly in minimum time. The judgement will be based on time taken and accuracy.

TIC-TECH-TOE

Introduction:

The classic way to get past from boredom: Tic-Tech-Toe reinvented. The one where we use crosses and zeroes to align in a way that makes a match. Why not to bring in electronics here? One´s capability to design the algorithms, code and implement on hardware is the need of the competition. This competition lays the perfect battleground for impassioned minds. So prove your caliber in the thrilling platform of Embedded Design Challenge.

Problem Statement:

The participants are required to implement the Tic-Tech-Toe game on the LCD/LED with an array size of 3X3.
Instructions:
1. Bi-colour LEDs(in case the design is implemented on led array) should be used to represent zeroes and crosses.
2. There should be a scoreboard designed through 7 segment led.
3. The game can be played in 2 modes:

a. Multiplayer mode
b. Single player mode, where the other player has to be a CPU. (This mode will get extra weightage, however it is not compulsory)
4. After a match of 3 zeroes or crosses is made, only the matched LEDs should blink for 3 times with a gap of one second.
5. Adding extra features to the game will provide the team with more marks, consequently chances to win will increase.

ASME

Junkyard Mania

This event is inspired from the popular show on the Discovery channel, Junkyard Wars. As the name itself suggest, the participant has to meticulously utilise the junks made available to them to make something constructive and innovative as per required by the problem statement, which will be disclosed on the spot.

Goldberg Conundrum

In this event, a simple task is made more complex by increasing the number of steps to accomplish the task. The participants will be given the problem statement well in advance and they have to accomplish that in the given constraint of minimum number of steps. The steps can be of variety of types depending upon the innovative skills of the participant.

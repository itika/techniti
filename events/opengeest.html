InQuest

InQuest is a quiz contest which will be held in 2 levels and will be conducted online as well as offline:
Level 1:
Level 1 would consist of questions of different formats like:-
•Mind benders
•Jumbled words (with a sentence given which will provide hint to the answer, the jumbled word will provide the answers).
•General questions related to internet /computer.
Level 2:
Participants will need nothing more than a good grey cell count to clear this level of contest. Teams/individuals will need to solve a crossword puzzle with the words they will get after solving 7 different puzzles.

Command Line Explorer

A series of tasks shall be given to the participants which they´ll complete using LINUX terminal. Participants will be shown a demonstration of various Linux commands and participants will be restricted to use only those commands in the complete contest.
